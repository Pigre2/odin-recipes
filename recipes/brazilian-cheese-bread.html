Brazilian Cheese Bread

Description

Yummy gluten-free and wheat-free breads, specially popular in the Brazilian

state of Minas Gerais.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil or butter
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/3 cup milk or soy milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 2/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C (375 degrees F).
  2. Mix oil, water, milk and salt in a large saucepan and place over high heat. Bring to a boil and immediately remove from the heat.
  3. Stir in tapioca flour and garlic, stirring until smooth. Let it rest for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Stir cheese and eggs into tapioca mixture until combined; the dough will be chunky, like cottage cheese.
  5. Drop dough by 1/4 cup size balls onto a baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the tops are lightly browned, 15 to 20 minutes.
