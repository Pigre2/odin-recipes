Caipirinha

Description

The Brazilian national cocktail is refreshing and delicous. Drink

responsibly.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lime, quartered
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 and 1/2 fluid ounces cachaça (Brazilian rum)
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Steps

  1. Squeeze and drop lime wedges into a large rocks glass. Add sugar and muddle until juices release.
  2. Pour in cachaça and ice. Stir well.
