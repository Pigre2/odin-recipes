Brigadeiro

Description

A yummy Brazilian dessert made of condensed milk and chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Mix condensed milk, cocoa, and butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until thickened, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let rest until mixture is cool enough to handle.
  2. Use your hands to shape into small balls and serve. Can be eaten at once or chilled until serving.
